Audio-Reactive TV

Solo Project - Procedural Art

For the course Procedural Art, our assignment was a lightly interactive and auditive experience, using procedural art techniques. I created an interactive TV, with the intention of recreating the nostalgic feeling of being completely immersed while watching TV. The TV explores several post-processing styles on the same scene, while having audio reactive features.
