Ans

Group project - Project Hybrid

Our assignment for this project was to create an interactive installation with both physical and digital components. Our experience was about the journey of ‘Ans’, an elderly woman with a fear of death. The installation contained a projected digital portrait, custom-made interactive controllers and a decorated environment.

My role in this project was developer / technical artist.

It was important for the 'chambers' that they appeared to be inside the small portraits that the beamer was projecting on. By using a stencil buffer, I was able to have the chambers appear only through the invisible plane in front of the objects.

My role in the creation of the portrait was developing the transition effects for the three scales, which can interact with the portrait.
