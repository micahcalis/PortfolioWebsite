Under Our Wings

Group project - Project Context II

The focus of this project was to create a game with the theme ‘sustainability’, it was also a collaboration with Utrecht University. Our concept was about social sustainability, specifically, the situation of refugees in Utrecht. The player is a pigeon that flies around the city, collecting stories from the refugees, and publishing those stories, consequently improving the state of the city.

My role in this project was gameplay designer, technical designer, technical artist and VFX artist.

For our map, we needed a tool that could paint textures on a plane, and be transparent near the canal. So, I designed a tool that interprets color information from a texture: red, green, blue and alpha, into (same order): texture type, tiling, rotation and transparency.

Input texture made by Gwenne Reiniers

We needed a system that could change the color and the detail of the houses on runtime, without having to make individual textures for each change. So, I made a shader that divides the UV’s of the houses into a set number of colors, and added detail by ‘’smoothstepping’ perlin noise.
