Boozy Boa

Group Project - Level Design

Despite the name of the course (which is a bit misleading) the assignment for this course is simply to publish a game. With Owen Boers, also a student at the HKU, we formed team ZestWave and created Boozy Boa. This game is heavily inspired by the original Snake game, but reimagines it into a comedic and modern environment.

Play!

These shader effects were made to make the game more difficult, and show the effect of the substances.
