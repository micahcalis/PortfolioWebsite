Artemis

Solo project - ISA

ISA stands for ‘Individuele Studie Activiteit’ (Individual Study Activity), where the assignment was to spend time developing a skill in an area of interest related to the bachelor. I chose to fully develop a character as a mod for Rivals of Aether. This included programming in GameMaker Studio, animating in Aseprite and designing an entire moveset for a character in a 2D platform fighter.
